<p><p>The smartphone and tablet display market is primarily categorized into various technologies: IPS (In-Plane Switching), TFT (Thin Film Transistor), OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode), and others. OLED displays provide vibrant colors and deep blacks, enhancing visual experiences. Other displays, such as Mini-LED and MicroLED, are emerging for their superior brightness and efficiency.</p></p>
