<p><p>The food-grade guar gum market is primarily segmented into two types: powder and solution. Guar gum powder is used for thickening, stabilizing, and improving texture in various food products, such as sauces, ice creams, and baked goods. The solution form, typically prepared by dissolving the powder in water, is utilized for applications requiring immediate viscosity, such as emulsions and dressings. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing food quality and consistency, catering to diverse culinary needs.</p></p>
