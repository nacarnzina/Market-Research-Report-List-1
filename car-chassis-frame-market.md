<p><ul><li>Load Bearing</li><li>Non-loaded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car chassis frame market can be categorized into two main types: load-bearing and non-loaded. Load-bearing chassis frames are designed to support the vehicle's weight and withstand dynamic loads, providing structural integrity and safety during operation. These frames are typically utilized in traditional vehicles that require high durability. In contrast, non-loaded chassis frames are used in lighter applications, such as certain electric vehicles or specialized designs, where they primarily serve as a support structure without bearing significant loads.</p></p>
