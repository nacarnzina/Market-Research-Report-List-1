<p><p>The dental CBCT market is categorized into three types based on field of view (FOV): Large, Medium, and Small FOV. Large FOV systems capture a broad area, making them ideal for comprehensive assessments of jaw, teeth, and surrounding structures, suitable for oral surgeons and orthodontists. Medium FOV is designed for specific regions, balancing detail and coverage, perfect for general practitioners. Small FOV focuses on localized areas, offering high-resolution images for procedures like endodontics and implantology, catering to precise diagnostics.</p></p>
