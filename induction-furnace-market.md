<p><strong>The Induction Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coreless Induction Furnace</li><li>Channel Induction Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The induction furnace market primarily comprises coreless and channel induction furnaces. Coreless induction furnaces use an electromagnetic field to heat the metal directly, allowing for efficient melting and flexibility in production capacities. They are widely used for steel and non-ferrous metal melting. On the other hand, channel induction furnaces feature a channel design that facilitates continuous operation for applications such as holding and pouring molten metal. Both types cater to diverse industries, including foundry, metallurgy, and recycling.</p></p>
