<p><p>The Dry Powder Fire Extinguishing Device market includes two primary types: Ordinary Dry Powder Fire Extinguishers and Ultrafine Dry Powder Fire Extinguishers. Ordinary dry powder extinguishers are effective for Class A, B, and C fires, utilizing a mixture of chemicals that suppress flames by forming a barrier between the fuel and oxygen. Ultrafine dry powder extinguishers use specially formulated powders that provide faster extinguishing capabilities and improved coverage, making them more efficient in combating fires, especially in industrial and high-risk environments.</p></p>
