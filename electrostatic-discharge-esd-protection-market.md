<p><p>The Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) Protection Market can be categorized into two main types: Ceramic Base and Semiconductor Base. Ceramic Base ESD protection devices utilize ceramic materials to dissipate electrostatic energy, offering high thermal stability and reliability in harsh environments. In contrast, Semiconductor Base ESD protection devices incorporate semiconductor technologies like diodes to provide efficient clamping of voltage spikes. Both types are essential for safeguarding electronic components from damage due to electrostatic discharge, ensuring the longevity and performance of electronic systems.</p></p>
