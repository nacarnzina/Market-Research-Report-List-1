<p><ul><li>Perforated Steamer Paper</li><li>Unpunched Steamer Paper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable steamer paper market consists of two main types: perforated steamer paper and unpunched steamer paper. Perforated steamer paper features small holes that allow steam to penetrate, ensuring efficient cooking while preventing food from sticking. This type is often preferred for its convenience and ease of use. Unpunched steamer paper, on the other hand, lacks these perforations, providing a more solid barrier, making it suitable for steaming delicate items without the risk of them breaking apart. Both types serve distinct culinary needs in food preparation.</p></p>
