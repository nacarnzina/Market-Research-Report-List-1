<p><strong>The Pneumatic Bolting Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tensioning Tool</li><li>Torque Tool</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic bolting tools market consists primarily of two types: tensioning tools and torque tools. Tensioning tools are used to apply a precise amount of tension to a bolt, ensuring even load distribution and reducing the risk of joint failure. In contrast, torque tools focus on delivering a specific amount of rotational force to tighten bolts, ensuring secure connections. Both types are essential in various industries, including construction, manufacturing, and automotive, where reliable fastening is critical for safety and performance.</p></p>
