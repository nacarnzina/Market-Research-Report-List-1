<p><ul><li>Dispersion Prism</li><li>Deviation Prism</li><li>Rotating Prism</li><li>Total Reflection Prism</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spectroscopic prism market includes various types, each serving distinct functions in optical applications. Dispersion prisms separate light into its component colors for analysis in spectroscopy. Deviation prisms alter the path of light without dispersion, useful in aligning instruments. Rotating prisms create dynamic effects by modifying light angles, enhancing visualization in displays. Total reflection prisms utilize the principle of total internal reflection, crucial for applications like fiber optics. Each type plays a vital role in optical analysis and instrumentation.</p></p>
