<p><p>Software Asset Management (SAM) services can be categorized into two main market types: On-Premises and Cloud-Based. </p><p>On-Premises SAM involves deploying software tools on a company’s local servers, providing greater control over data and compliance management but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based SAM solutions are hosted on external servers, offering scalability, ease of access, and automatic updates, typically reducing the need for extensive internal IT infrastructure. Both models aim to optimize software usage and ensure compliance with licensing agreements.</p></p>
