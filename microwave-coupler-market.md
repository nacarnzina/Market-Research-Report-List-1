<p><strong>The Microwave Coupler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coupled Transmission Line</li><li>Independent Transmission Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microwave coupler market includes two primary types: coupled transmission lines and independent transmission lines. Coupled transmission line couplers utilize interwoven transmission lines to efficiently transfer signals between circuits while maintaining desired coupling levels. In contrast, independent transmission line couplers utilize distinct, parallel lines for signal transmission, enabling flexibility and isolation between channels. Both types play crucial roles in applications like signal splitting, combining, and power measurement, catering to various demands in telecommunications and radar systems.</p></p>
