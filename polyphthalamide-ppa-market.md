<p><p>Polyphthalamide (PPA) is a high-performance polymer known for its thermal stability, chemical resistance, and mechanical properties. The market for PPA is primarily divided into two types: amorphous PPA and semi-crystalline PPA. Amorphous PPA exhibits excellent dimensional stability and clarity, making it suitable for applications requiring transparency. In contrast, semi-crystalline PPA offers enhanced strength and heat resistance, making it ideal for demanding environments. Both types are utilized in automotive, electronics, and industrial applications, driving growth in the PPA market.</p></p>
