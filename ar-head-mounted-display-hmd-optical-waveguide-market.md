<p><ul><li>Geometric Waveguide</li><li>Diffractive Waveguide (Surface Relief Grating)</li><li>Diffractive Waveguide (Volume Holographic Gratings)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AR Head-mounted Display (HMD) Optical Waveguide market consists of three primary types. Geometric waveguides utilize physical structures to direct light, enhancing image clarity and field of view. Diffractive waveguides with surface relief gratings manipulate light through micro-patterned surfaces, enabling thin designs with effective light management. Volume holographic gratings use layered holographic structures to project images, offering high efficiency and potentially wider angles. Each type serves to improve user experience by integrating digital elements into the real world seamlessly.</p></p>
