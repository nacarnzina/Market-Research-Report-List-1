<p><p>Treatment Planning Systems (TPS) are categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise systems. Cloud-based TPS is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering benefits like scalability, ease of collaboration, and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premise TPS is installed locally within a facility's infrastructure, providing greater control over data security and customization. Each type addresses different organizational needs, balancing flexibility and maintenance demands with control and data privacy considerations in healthcare settings.</p></p>
