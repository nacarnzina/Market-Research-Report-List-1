<p><p>The space-qualified diodes market encompasses various types tailored for aerospace applications. Schottky diodes offer low forward voltage drop, ideal for high-frequency operations. TVS diodes protect circuits by clamping voltage spikes. Switching diodes are used for signal modulation, and Zener diodes ensure voltage regulation. Each type ensures reliability and performance in the demanding space environment.</p></p>
