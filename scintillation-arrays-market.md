<p><p>Scintillation arrays are categorized based on the material used for photon detection in various applications. GAGG arrays utilize gadolinium aluminum gallium garnet for effective performance, while LYSO arrays, made from lutetium yttrium oxy-silicate, offer high density and light yield. BGO arrays, composed of bismuth germanate, provide good energy resolution. CsI arrays, using cesium iodide, are known for their excellent performance in medical imaging. CdWO4, CeBr3, and PbWO4 arrays are other specialized materials that enhance detection capabilities in specific applications, alongside various others available on the market.</p></p>
