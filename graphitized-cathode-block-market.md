<p><p>The Graphitized Cathode Block market is categorized into three main types: Semi-Graphitic, Graphitic, and Graphitized. Semi-Graphitic blocks contain a mix of carbon and graphite, offering a balance of conductivity and thermal resistance, making them suitable for certain industrial applications. Graphitic blocks are primarily composed of graphite, providing superior electrical conductivity and mechanical strength, ideal for high-performance settings. Graphitized blocks, created through a high-temperature treatment, enhance carbon materials' properties, resulting in advanced performance for extreme conditions in industries like aluminum production and steelmaking.</p></p>
