<p><p>The Fiber Optical Variable Attenuators (FOVAs) market is primarily divided into two types: Manual Fiber Optical Variable Attenuators and Electrical Fiber Optical Variable Attenuators. Manual FOVAs require user intervention for adjusting signal levels, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for applications where precision is less critical. In contrast, Electrical FOVAs utilize electronic controls for automatic attenuation, providing efficient and precise signal management in complex systems, making them suitable for high-performance networks and applications needing dynamic control over signal strength.</p></p>
