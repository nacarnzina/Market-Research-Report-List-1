<p><p>The High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP) spray guns market primarily includes two types: electrical airless spray guns and those that operate with air compressors. Electrical airless spray guns utilize electric power to atomize paint, providing a smooth finish with minimal overspray. Conversely, air compressor-based HVLP guns rely on compressed air to achieve fine atomization, allowing for better control and efficiency in paint application. Both types cater to various applications, from automotive to industrial coatings, emphasizing ease of use and precision.</p></p>
