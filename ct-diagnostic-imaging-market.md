<p><ul><li>Conventional CT scanners</li><li>Cone beam CT scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CT diagnostic imaging market comprises various types, primarily including conventional CT scanners and cone beam CT scanners. Conventional CT scanners provide detailed cross-sectional images and are widely used in hospitals for diagnosing various conditions. In contrast, cone beam CT scanners utilize a cone-shaped X-ray beam to produce 3D images, predominantly used in dental and maxillofacial imaging due to their ability to offer high-resolution images with lower radiation doses. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing diagnostic accuracy and treatment planning.</p></p>
