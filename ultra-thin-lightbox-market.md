<p><p>The ultra-thin lightbox market comprises three primary types based on lighting technologies: LED, EEFL, and T4 fluorescent bulbs. LED lightboxes are favored for their energy efficiency, longevity, and slim design, making them ideal for modern retail displays. EEFL (external electrode fluorescent lamp) lightboxes offer a broader light distribution and lower energy consumption compared to traditional fluorescent options. T4 fluorescent bulbs, characterized by their compact size and accessible cost, provide a practical solution for effective illumination in lightboxes, balancing performance and affordability.</p></p>
