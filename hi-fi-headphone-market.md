<p><p>The hi-fi headphone market is categorized into three primary types: in-ear headphones, on-ear headphones, and over-ear headphones. In-ear headphones are compact and portable, delivering sound directly into the ear canal, ideal for users seeking convenience and isolation. On-ear headphones sit on the outer ear, providing a balance between portability and sound quality. Over-ear headphones encompass the entire ear, offering superior sound immersion and comfort, often favored by audiophiles for extended listening sessions. Each type caters to distinct preferences and uses.</p></p>
