<p><ul><li>Open Barge</li><li>Covered Barge</li><li>Tank Barge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The barge transportation market includes three primary types: open barges, covered barges, and tank barges. Open barges are flat-bottomed vessels used for transporting bulk materials like coal or gravel, exposed to the elements. Covered barges, equipped with a protective canopy, transport goods sensitive to weather, such as agricultural products or construction materials. Tank barges are specialized for transporting liquids, including chemicals or petroleum products, ensuring safe and secure containment during transit. Each type caters to distinct cargo needs.</p></p>
