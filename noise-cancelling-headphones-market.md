<p><ul><li>Active Noise-Cancelling Headphones</li><li>Passive Noise-Cancelling Headphones</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The noise-cancelling headphones market is divided into two primary types: active and passive noise-cancelling headphones. Active noise-cancelling (ANC) headphones use electronic systems to detect ambient noise and generate sound waves that counteract it, effectively minimizing external sounds. In contrast, passive noise-cancelling headphones rely on physical barriers, such as cushioned ear cups, to block out noise through sound isolation. Both types cater to different user preferences, with ANC offering advanced sound suppression, while passive options are often more affordable and simpler in design.</p></p>
