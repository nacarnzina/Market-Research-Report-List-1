<p><ul><li>Hall Effect Joystick Handle</li><li>Potentiometer Joystick Handle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Multi Axis Joystick Handle market primarily comprises Hall Effect and Potentiometer joystick handles. Hall Effect joystick handles utilize magnetic fields for precise position sensing, offering durability and resistance to wear, making them ideal for demanding applications. In contrast, Potentiometer joystick handles rely on variable resistors to detect movement, providing straightforward and cost-effective solutions for less intensive use. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including gaming, robotics, and industrial control, addressing varying performance and pricing needs.</p></p>
