<p><p>The Low Soda Alumina Trihydrate (ATH) market is segmented into two main types: Dry ATH and Wet ATH. Dry ATH is produced through a drying process, resulting in a fine powder used primarily in plastics, rubber, and coatings for its flame-retardant properties. Wet ATH, on the other hand, retains moisture and is typically used in applications requiring higher purity and lower reactivity, such as in pharmaceuticals and cosmetics. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, emphasizing their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
