<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electronic Toll Collection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rfid-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Dsrc-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Video Analytic-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Gnss/Gps-Based ETC Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) System market comprises various technologies. RFID-based ETC systems utilize radio frequency identification for automatic vehicle identification and toll payment. DSRC-based systems rely on Dedicated Short-Range Communications for fast transactions, enabling real-time vehicle communication with tolling infrastructure. Video analytic-based systems employ cameras and advanced image processing to identify vehicles and process toll fees without requiring transponders. GNSS/GPS-based ETC systems use satellite positioning for location tracking and toll calculation, offering a flexible and accurate toll collection method.</p></p>
