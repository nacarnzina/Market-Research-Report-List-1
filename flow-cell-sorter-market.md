<p><p>The Flow Cell Sorter market is primarily segmented into two types: Fluorescent-activated Flow Cell Sorters and Magnetic-activated Flow Cell Sorters. Fluorescent-activated sorters utilize fluorescent markers to identify and separate cells based on specific characteristics, making them ideal for applications in cell biology and diagnostics. In contrast, Magnetic-activated sorters employ magnetic beads to isolate target cells from a sample, facilitating more refined sorting processes. Both technologies are essential in research, clinical diagnostics, and biomanufacturing, enhancing cell analysis capabilities.</p></p>
