<p><p>The flow cytometry instruments market is primarily categorized into cell analyzers and cell sorters. Cell analyzers are used for quantitative analysis of cell populations, measuring parameters like size, complexity, and fluorescence intensity, which helps in research and clinical diagnostics. Cell sorters, on the other hand, not only analyze but also physically separate specific cell types based on fluorescence and other properties, enabling targeted studies and applications in immunology, oncology, and genetics. Both types are vital in advancing cellular research.</p></p>
